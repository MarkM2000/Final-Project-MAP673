Outer Banks

This is the final project of the Outer Banks. I have created three mockups leading up to the this project. The first mockup was done by sketch, my second mockup was done by Adobe Illustrator, and the third was done with a mockup index. The final project is focused on the Outer Banks, and I will use everything that I learned with each of the mockups.

Features

The final project will feature hotels, lighthouses, and other tourist destinations. The final project will use svg files to represent the lighthouses and placemarks to represent the hotels. This will show vacation places in the Outer Banks, and I hope to visit the Outer Banks in the timeframe between 2024 and 2028 (which means I hope to visit in one of those years).
