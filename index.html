Outer Banks Mockup

This is the third mockup sketch of what my final project of the Outer Banks would look like. My first mockup was done by sketch, my second mockup was done by Adobe Illustrator, and this one will be done via index. The final project will be focused on the Outer Banks, which is the reason I did this mockup. This mockup features placemarks that represents the hotels and circles that represents the lighthouse, although changes will be made to it in the final project.

Planned features

The final project will feature tourist destinations such as hotels and lighthouses. In this mockup, the placemarks represent the hotels and the circles represent the lighthouses, and both icons will likely be changed. I will have a data source on which roads I plan to use for the final project. This will show vacation places in the Outer Banks, and I hope to visit the Outer Banks in the timeframe between 2024 and 2028 (which means I hope to visit in one of those years).
